Further webchat issues

 Martinp23 on 2010-10-18

Un­for­tu­nate­ly, it seems the box our we­bchat is on has de­cid­ed to fall out with the In­ter­net again. We're work­ing on set­ting up a re­serve in­stance which should­n't be af­fect­ed by this sort of is­sue in the fu­ture. When we have more de­tails, we'll up­date this post.
