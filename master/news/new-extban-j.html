New extban: $j

 Pricey on 2014-06-18

We have loaded a new mod­ule on the net­work which pro­vides the $j extban type:

$j:<chan> - match­es users who are or are not banned from a spec­i­fied chan­nel

As an ex­am­ple...

/mode #here +b $j:#tim­buk­tu

...would ban users from #here that are banned (+b) in #tim­buk­tu.

Please note that there are a cou­ple of gotchas:

  • Only match­ing +b list en­tries are checked. Qui­ets (+q) Ex­emp­tions (+e) & in­vex­es (+I) are NOT then con­sid­ered. As such, the fol­low­ing mode change would not al­ter the be­hav­iour of the first ex­am­ple:

    /mode #tim­buk­tu +e *!*@*

  • Qui­ets and the qui­et­ing ef­fect of bans may not im­me­di­ate­ly take ef­fect on #here when #tim­buk­tu's ban list changes due to caching by the ircd.

  • $j is­n't re­cur­sive. Any $j extbans set in #tim­buk­tu are ig­nored when match­ing in #here.

We imag­ine you'll have some more use­ful use cas­es than the above.

Thanks for fly­ing freen­ode!
