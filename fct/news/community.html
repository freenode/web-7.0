freenode Community Team

 Swant on 2016-10-14

We would like to help make your project more vis­i­ble on freen­ode, as a part of this we would like to in­tro­duce our­selves: Swant, mquin and Myrt­ti. We are the com­mu­ni­ty team, and if your project is go­ing to make a re­lease or up­date, hold an event or con­fer­ence or your project is ap­proach­ing a ma­jor mile­stone we’d love to an­nounce it on freen­ode and re­share your so­cial me­dia posts about it. So feel free to drop any of us a mes­sage on irc or email com­mu­ni­ty at freen­ode dot net.

For the rest of you, set umode +w to see all those awe­some news!
