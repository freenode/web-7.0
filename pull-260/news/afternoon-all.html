[Announcement] DNS change

 christel on 2005-12-16

freen­ode is cur­rent­ly ex­pe­ri­enc­ing a large-s­cale nui­sance­bot in­fes­ta­tion. We've tem­porar­i­ly re­moved the irc.freen­ode.net ro­ta­tion, but you can find us on chat.freen­ode.net. If you're a client de­vel­op­er or you run an IRC net­work list site, please do not list this new host­name with­out first email­ing staff at freen­ode dot net to make sure it's okay to do so. Apolo­gies for the in­con­ve­nience, and thank you for us­ing the net­work!
