[Announcement] PDPC Fundraiser

 christel on 2005-01-23

Good evening to every­one. Here's hop­ing you're hav­ing an ex­cel­lent new year. In the next day or so, Peer-Di­rect­ed Projects Cen­ter, the IRS 501(c)(03) parent organization of freen­ode, will begin its fundraising campaign for the fiscal year ending 30 June 2006. We're still working on budget estimates for FYE 2006, and we begin the campaign with about $2,440 in donations over and above those required for the current year.
